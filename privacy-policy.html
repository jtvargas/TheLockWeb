Privacy Policy for THE LOCK

Last Updated: November 9, 2023

At THE LOCK, we respect the privacy of our users. Our Privacy Policy explains how we deal with your information in the game.

As THE LOCK operates entirely locally on your device, we do not collect, store, or process any personal data or sensitive information from our users.

2. Data Usage

Since THE LOCK does not collect any personal data, there is no usage of user data for any purposes, including advertising or analytics.

3. Third-Party Services

THE LOCK does not include any third-party services that collect, store, or process personal data.

4. Data Security

As we do not collect any personal data through THE LOCK, there are no data security risks associated with the use of the game.

5. Children's Privacy

Since no personal data is collected, THE LOCK is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). It is safe for children to use without risk of data collection.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
